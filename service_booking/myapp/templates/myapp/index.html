Tire Repair and Replacement

You can’t ignore tread punctures or penetrations because they can cause irreversible tire damage. At the same time, an improper repair can damage the tire . Only punctures in the tread area less than ¼” in diameter may be potentially repaired and returned to service.

Wheel Alignment

Wheel alignment involves a mechanical adjustment of vehicle suspension to influence the direction and angle of the tyre's contact with the road surface. The optimum alignment for each make and model of vehicle is set by the manufacturer in order to influence the performance and handling.
